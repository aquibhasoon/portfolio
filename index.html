My Projects

RFID Service Managment

This project involves the development of an RFID-based service management system, designed to streamline operations and improve efficiency. The system uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology to track and manage assets, personnel, or inventory in real-time. By integrating RFID tags and readers, it automates tasks such as asset tracking, inventory management, and access control. The system aims to enhance data accuracy, reduce manual labor, and provide a seamless experience for managing resources across various services or industries.

Quantum Computing

This project focuses on learning the basics of quantum computing, including quantum mechanics, quantum gates, and simple quantum algorithms. Though still in the early stages, the goal is to establish a foundational understanding of quantum concepts.
